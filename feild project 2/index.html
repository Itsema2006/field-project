The Challenge

Protecting Harvests, Ensuring Livelihoods

Significant Crop Losses

Plant diseases annually account for substantial agricultural losses, threatening food security and farmer incomes globally.

Labor-Intensive Diagnosis

Current methods rely on manual inspection, which is time-consuming and requires specialized knowledge.

Lack of Accessibility

Many smallholder farmers lack access to expert agriculturalists or rapid diagnostic tools.
